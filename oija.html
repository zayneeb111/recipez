prep time

15 mins

cook time

25 mins

serving

4 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the vegetables

  1. Chop the tomatoes, onion, bell pepper, and garlic into small pieces.
  2. Slice the sausage (optional) if you're using it in the recipe.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.

Step 2: Sauté the vegetables

  1. Add the onion and garlic to the pan and sauté until soft and golden brown.
  2. Next, add the bell peppers and sauté for another 3 minutes.
  3. Then, add the chopped tomatoes, paprika, cumin, and chili flakes (or harissa) and cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.

Step 3: Add eggs

  1. Make wells in the vegetable mixture and crack the eggs into each well.
  2. Cover the pan and cook until the eggs are set but the yolks remain slightly runny (around 5-7 minutes).
  3. For a firmer texture, cook the eggs longer according to your preference.

Step 4: Garnish and serve

  1. Once cooked, garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot with a side of crusty bread.

Ingredients

4 large eggs

2 tomatoes (chopped)

1 onion (chopped)

1 bell pepper (chopped)

2 cloves garlic (minced)

2 tbsp olive oil

1/2 tsp paprika

1/2 tsp cumin

1/4 tsp chili flakes or 1 tbsp harissa

1 sausage (optional)

Fresh parsley (for garnish)

Salt and pepper to taste
