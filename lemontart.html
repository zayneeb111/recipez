Lemon Tart

prep time

30 mins

cook time

25 mins

serving

8 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and line a 9-inch tart pan with removable bottom.
  2. In a food processor, pulse the flour, powdered sugar, and salt together.
  3. Add the cold cubed butter and pulse until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.

step 2

  1. Gradually add the water and pulse until the dough comes together.
  2. Wrap the dough in plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Once chilled, roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface and press it into the tart pan.
  4. Use a fork to prick the bottom of the dough and bake for 15-20 minutes, or until lightly golden. Remove from oven and set aside to cool.

step 3

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, lemon juice, and zest until smooth.
  2. Pour the mixture into the cooled tart shell and bake for 10-12 minutes, until the filling is just set but still slightly wobbly in the center.
  3. Remove the tart from the oven and let it cool completely before serving.

ingredients

1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour

1/4 cup powdered sugar

1/4 tsp salt

1/2 cup unsalted butter, cubed

2 tbsp cold water

2 large eggs

3/4 cup granulated sugar

1/2 cup fresh lemon juice

1 tbsp lemon zest
