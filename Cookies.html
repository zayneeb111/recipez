Cheesecake Oreo Cookies

prep time

15 mins

cook time

25 mins

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Crush the Oreo cookies into fine crumbs using a food processor or rolling pin.
  3. Mix the Oreo crumbs with melted butter to form a crust.

step 2

  1. In a separate bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add sugar, vanilla, and eggs, mixing well after each addition.
  3. Fold in crushed Oreos for added texture.

step 3

  1. Pour the cheesecake batter over the Oreo crust.
  2. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until the cheesecake is set but still slightly jiggly in the center.
  3. Let it cool, then refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.
  4. Top with whipped cream or more crushed Oreos if desired.

ingredients

1 package Oreo cookies (about 20 cookies)

3 tbsp melted butter

16 oz cream cheese

1/2 cup sugar

1 tsp vanilla extract

2 large eggs
