Tunisian Lablebi

prep time

15 mins

cook time

45 mins

serving

4 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the chickpeas

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight, or use canned chickpeas if in a hurry.
  2. Drain and rinse if using canned chickpeas.

Step 2: Cook the chickpeas

  1. In a large pot, add the soaked chickpeas and cover with water.
  2. Bring to a boil, then simmer for about 30 minutes, or until chickpeas are tender.

Step 3: Prepare the broth

  1. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic, cumin, coriander, paprika, and harissa (if using) to the pan, and saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour this mixture into the pot with the chickpeas and add salt to taste.

Step 4: Serve the Lablebi

  1. Once the chickpeas are tender and the broth is flavorful, ladle the soup into bowls.
  2. Add a poached or fried egg to each bowl and drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Garnish with fresh cilantro, bread pieces, and extra harissa or lemon if desired.

Ingredients

1 cup dried chickpeas (or 2 cups canned chickpeas)

4 cups water

2 tbsp olive oil

2 cloves garlic (minced)

1 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp ground coriander

1 tsp paprika

1-2 tbsp harissa (optional, for spice)

Salt to taste

4 eggs (poached or fried)

Fresh cilantro for garnish

Torn bread pieces (for serving)

Olive oil (for drizzling)

Lemon wedges (optional, for serving)
