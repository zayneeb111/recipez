Tiramisu

prep time

20 mins

cook time

1 hour

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Start off by seperating the eggs
  2. Add sugar to the yolks
  3. Beat them together until mixture becomes pale in colour

step 2

  1. In another bowl ,add sugar to the egg whites
  2. Beat them until mixture doubles in volume
  3. Add your mascarpone to the egg yolk mixture
  4. Add one tsp of vanilla
  5. Add the whipped egg whites

step 3

  1. Soak ladyfingers in fresh coffee that has cooled down then place in your plate
  2. Add Cream and spread it out
  3. Add another layer of ladyfingers
  4. Add another layer of cream
  5. put it in the fridge for 1 hour
  6. Take it out and dust with cocoa powder or coffee powder

ingredients

225g of mascarpone

A packet of ladyfingers

2 eggs

40g sugar

Vanilla
