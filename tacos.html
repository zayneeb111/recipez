Homemade Saucy Tacos from Scratch

prep time

20 mins

cook time

30 mins

serving

4 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the tortillas

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, and water.
  2. Knead the dough for about 5 minutes until smooth.
  3. Divide into small balls and roll them out into thin circles.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium heat and cook the tortillas for about 1-2 minutes on each side, or until golden.

Step 2: Prepare the saucy filling

  1. In a pan, heat some oil and cook chopped onions, garlic, and bell peppers.
  2. Add your choice of protein (chicken, beef, or beans) and cook until done.
  3. Add taco seasoning and a bit of water to create a saucy mixture. Let it simmer until the sauce thickens.

Step 3: Assemble the tacos

  1. Place the cooked tortillas on plates.
  2. Spoon the saucy filling onto each tortilla.
  3. Top with shredded lettuce, cheese, sour cream, and salsa, or your favorite toppings.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Ingredients

2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tsp salt

3/4 cup warm water

1 tbsp olive oil

1 lb chicken or beef (or your choice of protein)

1 packet taco seasoning

1 cup salsa

Shredded lettuce, cheese, sour cream (optional toppings)
