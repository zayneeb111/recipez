Katsu Curry

prep time

15 mins

cook time

25 mins

serving

4 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the rice

  1. Cook the rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Set aside and keep warm.

Step 2: Prepare the chicken katsu

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Coat the chicken in flour, dip in beaten eggs, and then coat in panko breadcrumbs.
  3. Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat and fry the chicken for 4-5 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Remove from the pan and drain on paper towels. Slice the chicken into strips.

Step 3: Make the curry sauce

  1. In a separate pan, heat oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and cook until softened, about 3-4 minutes.
  3. Stir in the garlic and ginger, and cook for another 1 minute.
  4. Pour in the curry powder and cook for 1-2 minutes to release its flavors.
  5. Add the chicken stock and stir well. Let it simmer for about 10 minutes until thickened.
  6. Stir in the soy sauce, sugar, and mirin (if using), and cook for an additional 5 minutes until the sauce is rich and smooth.

Step 4: Serve the katsu curry

  1. Place a portion of rice on each plate.
  2. Top with sliced chicken katsu and pour the curry sauce over the top.
  3. Garnish with additional toppings like chopped green onions or sesame seeds, if desired.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Ingredients

4 chicken breasts

1 cup panko breadcrumbs

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

2 large eggs, beaten

2 cups rice

1 onion, chopped

2 garlic cloves, minced

1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated

2 tbsp curry powder

2 cups chicken stock

2 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp sugar

1 tbsp mirin (optional)

Vegetable oil for frying

Salt and pepper to taste
