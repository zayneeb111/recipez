Tunisian Mlewi

prep time

45 mins

cook time

1 hour

serving

6 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the dough

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, salt, sugar, and yeast.
  2. Add the warm water gradually and knead the dough until smooth.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes until it rises.

Step 2: Shape the dough

  1. Once the dough has risen, divide it into small balls.
  2. Flatten each ball into a round shape, approximately 1/4 inch thick.

Step 3: Cook the Mlewi

  1. Heat a flat griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Place each flattened dough piece onto the griddle and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side until golden brown and cooked through.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Serve the Mlewi warm, ideally with olive oil, honey, or cheese.
  2. Enjoy your Tunisian Mlewi as a delicious snack or side dish!

Ingredients

500g flour

1 tsp salt

1 tsp sugar

1 tsp active dry yeast

1/2 cup warm water (or more if needed)

Olive oil (for greasing)

Honey (optional, for serving)

Cheese (optional, for serving)
