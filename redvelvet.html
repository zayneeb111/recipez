Red Velvet Cake

prep time

20 mins

cook time

30 mins

serving

8 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch cake pans.
  2. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix together the sugar, eggs, buttermilk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract.

step 2

  1. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until smooth.
  2. Add the red food coloring and vinegar, and mix until fully combined.
  3. Divide the batter evenly between the two cake pans.

step 3

  1. Bake the cakes for 25-30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  2. Allow the cakes to cool in the pans for 10 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
  3. Once the cakes are cool, frost with cream cheese frosting (recipe below) and serve.

ingredients

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 tbsp cocoa powder

1 tsp baking soda

1 tsp salt

1 1/2 cups sugar

2 eggs

1 cup buttermilk

1/2 cup vegetable oil

1 tsp vanilla extract

2 tbsp red food coloring

1 tsp vinegar
