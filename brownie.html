Brownies

Fudgy Chocolate Brownies

prep time

15 mins

cook time

20mins

serving

6 servings

Brownie Chocolate Sweet

instructions

step 1 : Chocolate and butter

  1. Start of my adding your choclate into a bowl
  2. preheat oven to 180Â°C
  3. Add the Unsalted Butter
  4. Melt on the stove and keep stirring and set side to cool.

step 2 : wet ingredients

  1. In another bowl , add the eggs
  2. Add the sugar
  3. Use mixer to beat the eggs and sugar (important part to get the crackly top^$)
  4. Add the choclate mixture and mix
  5. Add 1 tsp of Vanilla

step 3 : Dry ingredients

  1. Add your flour
  2. Add cocoa powder
  3. 1/4 tsp of salt
  4. Gently fold that in
  5. Add chocolate chips , walnuts whatever you like
  6. Add your batter to a lined baking tray
  7. bake for 20-25 mins and keep an eye on them 8

ingredients

150g of chocolate (dark or milk)

100g of unsalted butter

2 eggs

150g sugar

Vanilla

60g all purpose flour

30g cocoa powder
