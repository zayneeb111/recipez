Creamy Gnocchi from Scratch

prep time

30 mins

cook time

30 mins

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Start by boiling the potatoes. Place them in a large pot, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Cook for 20 minutes or until tender.
  2. Once boiled, peel the potatoes while they are still warm. Mash them until smooth.

step 2

  1. Add flour, egg, and salt to the mashed potatoes. Mix until a dough forms.
  2. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and knead until smooth.
  3. Roll the dough into logs and cut into small pieces to form the gnocchi.

step 3

  1. Boil the gnocchi in salted water until they float, which will take about 2-3 minutes. Remove and drain.
  2. For the creamy sauce, heat butter in a pan. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant. Stir in cream and Parmesan cheese, cooking until the sauce thickens.
  3. Combine the cooked gnocchi with the creamy sauce, toss to coat, and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve with fresh herbs and extra grated Parmesan if desired.

ingredients

2 large potatoes

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour

1 egg

Salt

2 tbsp butter

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 cup heavy cream

1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Fresh herbs (optional)
