Lentils Spicy Dish

prep time

15 mins

cook time

25 mins

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Rinse the lentils thoroughly. In a large pot, add the lentils and cover with water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 10-15 minutes or until tender.

step 2

  1. While the lentils are cooking, heat oil in a separate pan over medium heat. Add chopped onions and saut√© until softened and golden.
  2. Add minced garlic, ginger, and chopped tomatoes to the pan. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the tomatoes break down and the mixture becomes fragrant.

step 3

  1. Add the cooked lentils to the pan with the tomato mixture. Stir to combine.
  2. Season with chili powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander. Stir well and let it simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  3. If the mixture becomes too thick, add a little water to reach your desired consistency.
