Spicy Salmon with White Rice

prep time

10 mins

cook time

15 mins

serving

2 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Bring the rice and water to boil for 15 mins until cooked

step 2

  1. In a small bowl, combine the chili paste, soy sauce, honey, garlic, and ginger. Mix well to create the spicy sauce.
  2. Season the salmon fillets with salt and pepper on both sides.

step 3

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Place the salmon fillets in the pan, skin-side down, and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  2. During the last minute of cooking, brush the salmon fillets with the spicy sauce mixture and cook for an additional minute to let the sauce caramelize.

step 4

  1. To serve, plate the white rice and top with the spicy salmon. Garnish with chopped green onions and sesame seeds for extra flavor and texture.

ingredients

2 salmon fillets

1 cup white rice

2 tbsp chili paste

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp honey

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tsp fresh ginger, grated

Olive oil for cooking

Salt and pepper to taste

Chopped green onions (optional, for garnish)

Sesame seeds (optional, for garnish)
