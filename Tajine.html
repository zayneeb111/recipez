Tunisian Lamb Tajine with Eggs

prep time

20 mins

cook time

1.5 hours

serving

6 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the ingredients

  1. Cut the lamb into bite-sized pieces and season with salt, pepper, and cumin.
  2. Chop the onions, garlic, and tomatoes.
  3. Whisk the eggs in a bowl with a little bit of milk (optional), salt, and pepper.

Step 2: Cook the lamb

  1. In a large pan, heat some olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the lamb pieces and brown them on all sides.
  3. Once browned, add the chopped onions, garlic, and tomatoes. Cook until softened.

Step 3: Add spices and simmer

  1. Add a pinch of saffron (optional), paprika, coriander, cumin, and cinnamon to the pan.
  2. Pour in enough water to just cover the lamb. Let it simmer for 1 hour, until the lamb is tender.

Step 4: Add the eggs

  1. Once the lamb is tender, pour the whisked eggs over the lamb and vegetable mixture.
  2. Cover the pan and cook on low heat for another 10-15 minutes, until the eggs are set and slightly golden on top.

Step 5: Serve

  1. Once the eggs are set, serve the Tajine hot, garnished with fresh cilantro or parsley.
  2. Enjoy with warm bread or couscous!

Ingredients

500g lamb (cut into pieces)

2 onions (chopped)

3 cloves garlic (minced)

2 tomatoes (chopped)

4 eggs

2 tbsp olive oil

1 tsp cumin

1 tsp paprika

1/2 tsp cinnamon

1/2 tsp coriander

A pinch of saffron (optional)

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh cilantro or parsley for garnish
