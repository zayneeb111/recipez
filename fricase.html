Tunisian Fricassé

prep time

30 mins

cook time

45 mins

serving

4 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the ingredients

  1. Boil the eggs and set them aside.
  2. Slice the Tunisian Fricassé bread into small sections.
  3. Prepare the vegetables and tuna, and shred the lettuce.
  4. Make the harissa sauce (optional) by blending the ingredients into a paste.

Step 2: Fill the bread

  1. Cut a small hole in the middle of each piece of bread.
  2. Stuff the bread with the tuna, boiled egg, tomatoes, and lettuce.

Step 3: Add the sauces

  1. Drizzle the harissa sauce (or your choice of sauce) into the bread with the fillings.
  2. Optionally, add a bit of olive oil and salt to taste.

Step 4: Serve

  1. Once filled, serve the Fricassé immediately while the bread is still fresh.
  2. Enjoy this delicious Tunisian street food!

Ingredients

4 pieces of Tunisian Fricassé bread

2 boiled eggs

1 can of tuna (preferably in olive oil)

1 tomato (sliced)

Lettuce (shredded)

Harissa sauce (optional)

Olive oil

Salt and pepper to taste
