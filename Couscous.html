Tunisian Lamb Couscous

prep time

30 mins

cook time

2 hours

serving

6 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the lamb

  1. Season the lamb with salt, pepper, and spices (cumin, coriander, paprika, and cinnamon).
  2. In a large pot, heat some olive oil and brown the lamb pieces on all sides.
  3. Add chopped onions, garlic, and cook until soft and fragrant.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, and a little water to create the base for the sauce.

Step 2: Cook the lamb

  1. Add carrots, zucchini, and chickpeas to the pot, and cook for 1 hour on low heat.
  2. Top up with more water or broth as needed and simmer until the lamb is tender.

Step 3: Prepare the couscous

  1. Steam the couscous in a couscoussier or a steamer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. After steaming, fluff the couscous with a fork and drizzle with olive oil.

Step 4: Combine and serve

  1. Place the couscous on a large serving dish and make a well in the center.
  2. Place the lamb and vegetable stew on top of the couscous.
  3. Garnish with cilantro and serve with a side of harissa or hot sauce if desired.

Ingredients

1 kg lamb (cut into chunks)

2 tbsp olive oil

1 large onion (chopped)

2 cloves garlic (minced)

3 tomatoes (chopped)

1 tbsp tomato paste

2 carrots (sliced)

2 zucchinis (sliced)

1 can chickpeas (drained)

Spices: cumin, coriander, paprika, cinnamon, salt, and pepper

2 cups couscous

Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Harissa or hot sauce (optional)
