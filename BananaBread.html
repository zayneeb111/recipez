Banana Bread

prep time

15 mins

bake time

30 mins

serving

8 servings

instructions

step 1

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until smooth.
  3. Mix in the melted butter into the mashed bananas.

step 2

  1. Stir in the baking soda, salt, and vanilla extract.
  2. Add the beaten egg and sugar, mixing well.
  3. Gradually add the flour, stirring until just combined.

step 3

  1. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and smooth the top.
  2. Bake for 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  3. Let it cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

ingredients

3 ripe bananas

1/2 cup melted butter

1 tsp baking soda

Pinch of salt

1 tsp vanilla extract

1 egg, beaten

3/4 cup sugar

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
