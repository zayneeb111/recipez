Pepperoni Pizza

prep time

15 mins

cook time

20 mins

serving

4 servings

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the dough

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add warm water and olive oil, and stir until a dough forms.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in an oiled bowl, cover it, and let it rise for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Step 2: Preheat the oven

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).

Step 3: Shape and add toppings

  1. Once the dough has risen, punch it down and divide it into two portions (for two pizzas).
  2. Roll out the dough into a circle and place it on a greased baking sheet or pizza stone.
  3. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Top with slices of pepperoni.

Step 4: Bake the pizza

  1. Bake the pizza in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  2. Remove the pizza from the oven, let it cool for a couple of minutes, then slice and serve.

Ingredients

2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour

1 packet active dry yeast

1 tsp sugar

1 tsp salt

1 cup warm water

1 tbsp olive oil

1/2 cup pizza sauce

2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Pepperoni slices (about 1 cup)
